Manifesto

Mankind is becoming ever integrated with technology.

Moore's law is relentless and visions of a "technological singularity" seem more compelling, year by year.

And yet, our progress towards that future is uneven and often faltering; how distressing it is that our knowledge is still full of holes!

As a computer programmer and entrepreneur, my hope is to wield technology in the fight to achieve the perfect union of human biology with technology.
